How To Use:

Designate canvas and icon sizes, and click either「Randomize」("r") or「Organize」("o").

Use commas (",") to separate multiple entries.

「Generate」("s") will generate images of the canvas.
Right-click to save. (Incompatible on some mobile devices.)
